Delicious Brownie Recipe

Indulge in the rich goodness of homemade brownies. Follow the recipe below to create a mouthwatering treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Butter, melted
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 cup Chocolate Chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, and sugar.
  3. Add the melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Transfer the batter to a greased baking pan and spread it evenly.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with moist crumbs.
  7. Allow the brownies to cool, then cut into squares and enjoy!
